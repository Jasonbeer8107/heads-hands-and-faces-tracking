Instructions:
Use preferably this program in a well
litted room. You will get better
performances in front light. Hand
tracking will work better if you
avoid complex or skin like colour
backgrounds and shadows on hands.

Commands:
f: To track or not faces
p: To track or not head pose
h: To track or not open hands
e: To track or not facial expressions
1 - 9: To substitute faces by models
0: No substitution
d: To freeze or not display
m: to display or not this menu
r: to reset settings

Put your own models in ./models
